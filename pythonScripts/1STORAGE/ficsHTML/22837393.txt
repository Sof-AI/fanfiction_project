        <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Work Text:</h3>
          <div class="userstuff"><p align="justify"><b>1. Yue Qingyuan, d</b><b>uring</b> <b> recover</b><b>y period</b><b>: </b> <b>M</b><b>aking </b> <b>Sword Noises </b> <b>With Your Mouth Is Better Than Making Gun Noises.</b></p><p align="justify">After Shen Qingqiu’s sudden fever, Yue Qingyuan had become sick with worry himself, staying at his bedside for hours on end, only forced away by the few odd disciples who were brave enough and an increasingly exasperated Mu Qingfang.</p><p align="justify">When Shen Qingqiu finally woke up, he was changed. Yue Qingyuan, as the person closest to him, immediately noticed it, quickly figuring out that the new personality was due to some sort of amnesia. In the end, he was too thankful that his dearest had woken up at all to be bothered at his change in character, and Mu Qingfang himself said it was harmless.</p><p align="justify">He patiently endured this new Shen Qingqiu’s careful questions, helped him gather his thoughts and get used to the life of a Peak Lord again. He even passed over the strange relationship Shen Qingqiu formed with one of his disciple, Luo Binghe.</p><p align="justify">…To be entirely honest, there was something entirely refreshing and, just a bit cute, about the way Shen Qingqiu acted now.</p><p align="justify">Upon one morning, only a few days after he’d awoken, Yue Qingyuan decided to visit him again.</p><p align="justify">As a regular visitor, Yue Qingyuan was used to not having to warn his arrivals. This time, Shen Qingqiu clearly did not remember that Yue Qingyuan was to visit that day, as no one came to welcome him. Curious and amused at this development, he headed into the bamboo house by himself.</p><p align="justify">Perhaps it was because he was still recovering, perhaps it was because Yue Qingyuan’s steps were too light—no matter the reason, when Yue Qingyuan entered, he found that Shen Qingqiu was completely unaware of his presence.</p><p align="justify">Indeed, Shen Qingqiu was holding his sword, swinging it around.</p><p align="justify">No finesse in his movement, one could even say that he seemed more like a young master trying to be impressive than a true master of this art. His expression was unrestrained, mouth in a small grin and eyes sparkling. Hair disheveled, clothes in disarray, skin just the slightest bit sweaty, he looked younger than ever.</p><p align="justify">Yue Qingyuan held a hand against his own mouth, hiding his smile.</p><p align="justify">Adorable! He wondered if a child Xiao Jiu would have acted like this, too.</p><p align="justify">He must have made a noise of amusement, because Shen Qingqiu suddenly flinched and whirled around. “S-Sect Leader!” Immediately, his expression went neutral, although a perturbed twitch to his eyes left the impression of embarrassment. He adopted a collected posture, which was immediately betrayed by his untidy appearance. “This shidi did not expect shixiong to visit today…”</p><p align="justify">Were his ears blushing? Truly, not unlike a child caught during wrongdoings…</p><p align="justify">Since Shen Qingqiu clearly wanted to avoid the issue, Yue Qingyuan thought it best to not anger him. He smiled calmly. “Apologies to Qingqiu-shidi.”</p><p align="justify">Apologies to Qingqiu-shidi, for catching him unaware! If only he could have remained hidden for just a few more seconds…</p><p align="center">.</p><p align="justify"><b>2. Mu Qingfang, after recovery: </b> <b>Disney </b> <b>P</b><b>rincesses </b><b>Influence </b> <b>The Youth Yet Again.</b></p><p align="justify">Mu Qingfang was supposed to have weekly visits from Shen Qingqiu, in order to check-up on how his body was doing after his severe fever. However, his peak was often busy, and he arrived late at one of said check-ups.</p><p align="justify">When he entered, Shen Qingqiu was already there, and seemed to have been waiting for a while. His fan was laid onto the bed, while he was standing in the middle of the room.</p><p align="justify">He was twirling, watching as the bottom of his robes followed the movement. His expression was guiltily pleased. In this moment, he looked more like a young maiden trying out the flow of her new skirt than a refined cultivator.</p><p align="justify">Eventually, Shen Qingqiu glanced up and saw that someone was at the doorway.</p><p align="justify">Shen Qingqiu clearly startled at seeing Mu Qingfang here, though he valiantly tried to hide his shock. However, Mu Qingfang was used to noticing all physical reactions, and saw his body jolt. Shen Qingqiu quickly showed a neutral face, muscles tensing then relaxing as though nothing was wrong. “Mu-shidi, this shixiong did not expect you here.”</p><p align="justify">“Shixiong,” Mu Qingfang greeted back. “This shidi is here for the last check-up.” Although he was aware that Shen Qingqiu was internally embarrassed at being caught, he was too curious and asked: “Is there an issue with shixiong’s robes?”</p><p align="justify">Shen Qingqiu jerked his head like he wanted to turn away, then remembered he was supposed to be calm and collected and forced his expression to remain as neutral as possible. “No issue. This shixiong was only…” His face truly went red, then.</p><p align="justify">“Shixiong was only…?”</p><p align="justify">“Only trying out…”</p><p align="justify">“Only trying out?”</p><p align="justify">Seeing that Mu Qingfang would not give up, Shen Qingqiu was forced to say: “Only trying out the twirl.” As soon as the words had slipped out, his mouth parted in shock and he whirled around to hide his face, no doubt now unable to keep his composure.</p><p align="justify">Mu Qingfang found that this version of his shixiong was adorable. He suddenly felt that he had tricked a child into admitting something against their will.</p><p align="justify">He smiled pleasantly. “Of course.” Then, deciding not to embarrass his shixiong further, he moved on and began his check up, all the while secretly enjoying his shixiong’s red cheeks and ears.</p><p align="justify">He hoped he would see more of this side of his shixiong in the future.</p><p align="center">.</p><p align="justify"><b>3. Liu Qingge, after poisoning: Simple Excitement </b> <b>Does Not Equate To Childishness, You’re Just Dull, Qingge</b><b>.</b></p><p align="justify">Even after his life had been saved by Shen Qingqiu, Liu Qingge could not say with all his heart that they were to be called friends. Although he had heard from Yue Qingyuan about his recent change in character and lack of memory, it was not like it completely erased his past actions.</p><p align="justify">Still, he could not deny that this Shen Qingqiu was… different, to say the least.</p><p align="justify">After Shen Qingqiu had been poisoned, following the demon invasion attempt on Cang Qiong, Liu Qingge felt indebted to him, having his life saved by this person. Naturally, as a righteous cultivator, he decided to repay his debt.</p><p align="justify">And so, he began to spend more time with Shen Qingqiu, not only to aid with his recovery, but also to observe his new personality.</p><p align="justify">He soon noticed that Shen Qingqiu got… overly excited about certain things.</p><p align="justify">Whenever Liu Qingge mentioned a magical creature, he would hurry to ask a thousand questions, even though he seemed to already know the answers and only wanted someone to confirm. Sometimes, if Liu Qingge himself did not know the answer, Shen Qingqiu would blurt out all the characteristics of this beast, with a smug, prideful expression.</p><p align="justify">Of course, he still showed a collected, calm appearance at all times, yet at the end of his rants, there always was a flush to his cheeks and a smile on his lips—an unsteady rhythm to his breath, with how fast the words poured out.</p><p align="justify">Liu Qingge was reminded of his little sister, when she had been younger, each time. He could not help but wonder if Shen Qingqiu was younger than him or not.</p><p align="center">.</p><p align="justify"><b>4. Qi Qingqi, before tournament: </b> <b>Only The Weak Don’t Like Sweets.</b></p><p align="justify">She had heard of the fever, of course, and Yue Qingyuan had warned them to be careful with this new version of Shen Qingqiu, but she had never expected…</p><p align="justify">That time Shen Qingqiu asked to join her carriage and even dared to invite his favored disciple inside along with him, Qi Qingqi simply did not know what to make of him.</p><p align="justify">When he offered her the sweets made by said disciple, she had no words. Was the way he half-closed his eyes in bliss when biting into a sweet a trick? What about his innocent, expectant expression, holding out the sweet in his palm for her to pick!?</p><p align="justify">Why was Shen Qingqiu, that scum, suddenly so sweet!?</p><p align="justify">She accepted the sweet with numb fingers, muttering against her will: “Many thanks.”</p><p align="justify">Shen Qingqiu smiled, nodded, and hid his mouth behind his fan again. The way he slipped sweets behind his fan, still hiding his face… Like a young master hiding the sweets he stole from the kitchen, not wanting to be scolded by his mother…</p><p align="justify">Qi Qingqi sighed. Perhaps she was getting too far in her fantasy.</p><p align="justify"><em> But still… </em> She observed Shen Qingqiu’s happily curved eyes. He could do with being a little more self-aware… At this rate he’ll act more childish than his own students!</p><p align="center">.</p><p align="justify"><b>5. Shang Qinghua, after abyss: </b> <b>What Do You Mean An Underage Kid Managed To Read My 18+ Novel!?</b></p><p align="justify">After they both realized they were transmigrators, Shang Qinghua and Shen Qingqiu got together to discuss how things were going and what were their plans for the future.</p><p align="justify">Midway through, Shang Qinghua exclaimed: “And what’s with the way you’re acting? So OOC! Even Liu Qingge has noticed that you’re being childish.”</p><p align="justify">Shen Qingqiu flushed red, waving his fan threateningly. “I’m not a child! I’m just— I’ve always been a xianxia enthusiast, so it’s understandable that I would act a bit OOC when I have the chance to experience it.”</p><p align="justify">But Shang Qinghua was not listening. His eyes had gone wide at the first protest. Weakly: “Bro… Cucumber bro… Just how old are you again?”</p><p align="justify">Clearly displeased, Shen Qingqiu frowned. “Sixteen. Why?”</p><p align="justify">Shang Qinghua went white.</p><p align="justify">Shen Qingqiu was younger than his own students!?</p><p align="center">.</p><p align="justify">
  <b>Bonus. Shen Qingqiu, in limbo: O</b>
  <b>bliviousness Is Not A Good Character Trait.</b>
</p><p align="justify">Just as one would expect, Shen Qingqiu was affronted at the thievery of both his body and his name, but in the end he was unable to do anything about it. Instead, after the fever that stole his life, he was forced into limbo, watching over his replacement’s actions.</p><p align="justify">Of course, in the beginning he was angered enough to wish to destroy that replacement soul with his bare hands, but as time went on…</p><p align="justify">He realized that this person was just an oblivious child!</p><p align="justify">From the dreams of his old life they shared, this person’s name was Shen Yuan. He was a sixteen year old child, and clearly not qualified to be a teacher to brats who were almost all older than him! He thought cultivation and magical beasts were impressive in the way an uneducated child might, and often acted foolish and childish and was caught during it.</p><p align="justify">Sixteen years old!</p><p align="justify">Shen Qingqiu felt he had no more face, having to see Yue Qingyuan’s and Mu Qingfang’s and Liu Qingge’s indulgent faces! Even Qi Qingqi showed a fond expression now!</p><p align="justify">And this Shang Qinghua… If only he’d known before his death that this person was responsible for all his suffering…! And now was letting this child be a teacher! He would have wrung out his neck by himself, no matter if their martial siblings tried to protest!</p><p align="justify">As for Shen Yuan…</p><p align="justify">Shen Qingqiu could not truly be resentful of him. This child was not that smart—or at the very least, was easily manipulated, whether it be by this System of his, his old family, his new disciples, or Shen Qingqiu’s martial siblings. Even Yue Qingyuan thought himself grand enough that he coerced Shen Yuan in acting cute! This was still Shen Qingqiu’s body, how dare they!</p><p align="justify">The child was clearly trying to do his best, but was used to a very different society. Still, would it kill him to be a little more self-aware!? Stop ruining Shen Qingqiu’s image!</p></div>
