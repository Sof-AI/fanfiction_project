Weird.

Phil looks him in the eye, waiting. But there is a fucking limit to how far they can skate on the whole Dead Mom/Wife Thing and this is it.

Like finding a job. And making sure that nobody’s squatting in his apartment. 

Phil’s face does something...weird.

“You know we love you,” he say. 

Oh yeah, here it comes. Tommy’s heard this one before. 

“You’re a delight to have around mate,” Phil continues. 

His clothes are probably washed by now, he can grab Henry out of Pihl’s room and change in there. Give Techno back his robe.

Did he bring his phone? He doesn’t remember having it but he does sort of remember Phil asking him to unlock it? Maybe? Was that just a fever dream? 

“Will has been so much happier since he found you, Techno too, and me. You make us all so happy, Toms.” 

So, clothes, Henry, blanket, possibly phone. Does he have shoes? He doesn’t remember. That’ll be a fucking pain to walk back to his apartment barefoot. 

“You fit in so well, like you were always meant to be here. You’re part of the family, I really, truly mean it.” 

Maybe he could borrow an old pair from Will? No, that’s probably asking too much. He definitely couldn’t afford to buy a pair off him, even used.

What?

Phil looks at him, his face is--is soft. And loving. And Tommy can’t help but flick a glance behind himself to see who he’s looking at. But there’s just Tommy. 

He leans forward and cups Tommy’s face in his hands. “We want you to stay, with us. Forever,” Phil says. “Be ours, Tommy.”

Wilbur makes a wounded noise from the couch beside him. 

“I do,” Phil says.

He’s not the kind of person people want to stay.  

“You are wonderful, ” Phil says, stroking a thumb over his cheek. “We love you. We want you to stay. We want you to be ours.” 

This is--

This is just mean.  

Tommy jerks away. “No. You’re--” He takes a deep breath. “You don’t--I’m not the person you want. You can find some other kid, you’ll like them better. I’m just gonna go.” 

He takes a step towards the door. 

Techno rises in front of him. 

“Tommy,” he says. 

“Techno, move.” Tommy mangages, keeping his eyes down. He can’t bear to look any of them in the face. He takes a step to the side. Techno matches him.
